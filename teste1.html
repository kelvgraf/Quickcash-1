Cash to

It is the transfer of money from one individual to another.

This is the classic pattern through which money can be transferred from one individual to another in just a few minutes. Generally, funds are paid in cash to the beneficiary and in the local currency of the receiving country. If desired, the sender can be notified by SMS when the funds are received by the beneficiary

How to make Cash to Cash transfer?

STEP 1 Find the nearest QuickCash agency to you. STEP 2 Visit the QuickCash agency with a valid identity card. STEP 3 Fill out the Sender Form and then submit it to the person behind the counter along with the money to be transferred and the transfer fees STEP 4 After entering all information and validating transaction, the operator will issue a receipt on which will appear a control number of 12 digits STEP 5 Communicate to the beneficiary of the transfer : -The control number of the transfer -The password used on the Sender Form -The amount to be received

How to receive money from a Cash to Cash transfer?

STEP 1 Ask the sender to communicate : -The control number -The password -The amount to be collected STEP 2 Go to a QuickCash agency with a valid identity card. Find a QuickCash agent STEP 3 Fill out the Receiver Form, and then give it to the person behind the counter. After verifying the transfer information and validation, the agent will give you the money in local currency.
